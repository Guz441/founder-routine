📅 Daily Ritual (6AM Mode)

  • 🚿 Wake up, cold face wash or shower
  • 🧘‍♂️ 5 mins breathing + "Why am I building this life?"
  • 📓 Write Today’s Mission (1-3 tasks)
  • 📵 Kill distractions — airplane mode ON
  • ⏱️ Deep Work Sprint #1 (50 min)
  • 💧 Hydrate + protein breakfast
  • ⏳ Deep Work Sprint #2
  • 📓 Write: What did I learn today?

🎯 Weekly Growth Tracker

  • 📖 What did I read this week?
  • 🛠️ What did I build?
  • 🧠 What concept did I master?
  • 💥 Where did I fail or feel resistance?
  • 🔁 What will I improve next week?
